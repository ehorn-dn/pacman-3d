HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

W / ↑Move Up
S / ↓Move Down
A / ←Move Left
D / →Move Right
CCycle Camera
MToggle Sound
HToggle Help

In third-person view, controls are relative to Pac-Man's facing direction.

RULES

  • Eat all dots to complete a level
  • Power pellets let you eat ghosts
  • Avoid ghosts — contact costs a life
  • You start with 3 lives

SCORING

  • Dot: 10 pts
  • Power Pellet: 50 pts
  • 1st Ghost: 200 pts
  • 2nd Ghost: 400 pts
  • 3rd Ghost: 800 pts
  • 4th Ghost: 1600 pts
